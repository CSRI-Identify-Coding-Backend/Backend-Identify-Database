QUESTIONs:

Explanation about the questionnaire, what to do; what to expect...

On the scale from 0 to 5 rated:

  • 0: Incomplete process - The process is not implemented or fails to achieve its purpose.
  • 1: Performed process - The implemented process achieves its process purpose.
  • 2: Managed process - The level 1 performed process is now implemented in a managed fashion (planned, monitored ans adjusted) and its work products are appropriately established, controlled and maintained.
  • 3: Established process - The level 2 managed process is now implemented using a defined process that is capable of achieving its process outcomes.
  • 4: Predictable process - The level 3 established process now operates within defined limits to achieve its process outcomes.
  • 5: Optimising process - The level 4 predictable process is continuosly improved to meet relavant current and projected business goals.
